Scenes

Music sits under your voice automatically (ducked low). Built-in beds are simple, copyright-safe tones — for the best sound, upload a real royalty-free track (e.g. from a free music library). Never use copyrighted songs — Meta flags them and it kills monetization.

Image Studio

Generate images only — then recreate, upscale, download, or turn any image into a free moving clip. Runs on the free Flux engine.

Recreate = a fresh take of the same prompt. Upscale 2× enlarges the image (free, in-browser). 🎬 Clip exports an MP4 with the camera motion + length you picked above — your free moving image. Note: this moves the camera over the picture; to make the subject actually move (walk/talk), use Animate (fal.ai · Pro) in the Video Generator.
